Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

4 August 2024

I want to bridge the gap between the technology law and tech start ups that are changing the world. In order to do so, I believe it’s crucially important to understand the basic work they do and the challenges they face. Immersion is key.

1. A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

  • Risk Taker - I get out of my comfort zone a lot. Learning to be uncomfortable is essential to development.
  • Self Disciplined & Independent - I’m self-disciplined enough to have structure to my life, plan ahead and work independently to achieve my goals without needing much external motivation.
  • Resilient – I thrive under complex problems and difficult circumstances.
  • Arrogant/Complacent – Overestimating time frames.
  • Growth Mindset – I’m always learning and developing.

2. What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Leadership – The ability to be competent enough to take care of yourself but also encourage and help others to achieve their goals.
